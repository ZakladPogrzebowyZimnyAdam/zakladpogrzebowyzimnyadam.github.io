HOME PAGE OF THE FUNERAL HOME

"Welcome to the website of Zimny Adam Funeral Home. We are an experienced and professional team that has been providing funeral services to residents of the city and surrounding areas for years. Our goal is to provide you and your loved ones with support at a difficult time after the loss of a loved one. "

We offer a wide range of services, including the organization of funeral ceremonies, transportation of the deceased, preparation of the deceased for burial and assistance with formalities. We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We encourage you to review our offer and contact us if you have any questions or concerns.
