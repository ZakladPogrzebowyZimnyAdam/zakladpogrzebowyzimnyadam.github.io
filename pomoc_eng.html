ASSISTANCE AFTER THE DEATH OF A LOVED ONE

WHAT YOU CAN DO AFTER THE DEATH OF A LOVED ONE:


-Contact family and friends for emotional support

-Don't be afraid to ask for help, both practical and emotional

-Make sure you have time to process your emotions and say goodbye to the person you lost

-Don't be afraid to seek professional help if you feel you are having a hard time coping with grief

-Be gentle with yourself and give yourself time to go through the grieving process.

YOU CAN ALSO:


-Contact the helpline 116-111

-Contact our corporate psychologist
