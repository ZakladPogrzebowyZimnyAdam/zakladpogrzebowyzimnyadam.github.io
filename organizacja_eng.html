GUIDE TO THE ORGANIZATION AND FUNERAL PROCESS


When we unfortunately have to face the death of a loved one, we often find it difficult to cope with all the decisions we have to make. To help you through this difficult process, we have prepared a guide to the funeral organization process that will help you go through it step by step:



Step 1: Death notification - the first step is to report the death to the relevant authorities and obtain permission for burial.

Step 2:: Choosing a funeral ceremony - next you need to decide whether you want a traditional burial, cremation or another ceremony.

Step 3:: Arrange transportation - you need to arrange transportation of the deceased to the funeral ceremony site.

Step 4:: Reserve a cemetery or crematorium space - you need to reserve a cemetery or crematorium space if you decide to have a burial or cremation.

Step 5:: Arrange the funeral ceremony - you need to organize the funeral ceremony, choose a casket or urn, arrange flowers, music, etc.

Step 6:: Arrange post-funeral care - after the funeral ceremony, you need to take care of post-funeral care such as grave care, consultation, etc.
