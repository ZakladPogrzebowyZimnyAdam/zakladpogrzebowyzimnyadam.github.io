INFORMATION ABOUT OUR COMPANY


"Zimny Adam Funeral Home is a family-owned business that has been in business for several generations. Our mission is to provide the highest quality funeral services to help families get through the difficult time of grief. We offer a full range of funeral services, including the organization of funeral ceremonies, preparation of the deceased for burial, as well as advice and assistance with paperwork. We are proud that our services have been recognized by various industry organizations, which have awarded us many certificates and awards for quality and professionalism. Our team is made up of experienced professionals who, with their commitment and empathy, help families get through difficult times. Our priority is to provide full support and assistance to each of our clients. We invite you to take advantage of our services and see our full offer on our website."
